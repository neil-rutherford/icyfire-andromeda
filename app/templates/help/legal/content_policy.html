Content Policy

Last updated February 17, 2021

Remember the human

Galatea is a place for creating community and belonging, not for attacking marginalized or vulnerable groups of people. Everyone has a right to use Galatea free of harassment, bullying, and threats of violence. Communities and users that incite violence or that promote hate based on identity or vulnerability will be banned.

Abide by the community rules

Post authentic content into communities where you have a personal interest, and do not cheat or engage in content manipulation (including spamming, view manipulation, ban evasion, or fraud) or otherwise interfere with or disrupt Galatea communities.

Respect the privacy of others

Instigating harassment, for example by revealing someone’s personal or confidential information, is not allowed. Never post or threaten to post intimate or sexually-explicit media of someone without their consent.

No child pornography

Do not post or encourage the posting of sexual or suggestive content involving minors.

No impersonation

You don’t have to use your real name to use Galatea, but don’t impersonate an individual or an entity in a misleading or deceptive manner.

Keep it legal

Keep it legal, and avoid posting illegal content or soliciting or facilitating illegal or prohibited transactions.

Don't break the site

Don’t break the site or do anything that interferes with normal use of Galatea.
