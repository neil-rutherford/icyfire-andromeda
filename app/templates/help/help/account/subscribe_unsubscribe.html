Subscribe to / unsubscribe from emails

Users may or may not want to receive daily email updates about what's new on Galatea. These email updates are turned off by default. To enable this functionality, you must first verify your email.

Subscribing to emails

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Click the link in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down to the "Email support" subsection of the "Manage your account" section. Click on the grey button.

Unsubscribing from emails

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Click the link in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down to the "Email support" subsection of the "Manage your account" section. Click on the grey button.
