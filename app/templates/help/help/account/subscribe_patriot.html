Subscribe to Patriot Tier

Subscribing to Patriot Tier can significantly enhance your Galatea experience. For just $5/month, you can get rid of ads, customize your profile, and write articles.

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Click the link in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down to the "Upgrade to Patriot Tier" subsection of the "Manage your account" section. Click the green button. This will take you to the Membership Pricing page.
  4. On the Membership Pricing page, click the green button.
  5. Enter your payment details, then click on the blue button.

If your subscription was successful, you will be redirected back to the "Account Settings" page with a message saying "Thank you for your business!". Payments will be automatically collected monthly.
