Unsubscribe from Patriot Tier

You are free to unsubscribe from a Patriot Tier subscription at any time for any reason.

What happens when I unsubscribe?

  • Your custom profile picture will be replaced with an anonymous avatar.
  • Your status will be blank.
  • You will lose your PATRIOT badge.
  • You will lose your ad-free experience.
  • You will still earn coins from articles that you wrote.

How do I unsubscribe from Patriot Tier?

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Click the link in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down to the "You're a Patriot!" subsection of the "Manage your account" section. Click the button.

If your unsubscription was successful, you will see a message saying "Your Patriot Tier subscription has been cancelled.".
