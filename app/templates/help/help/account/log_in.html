Log in

You need to be logged in to do certain things on Galatea, such as commenting on articles as well as interacting with other users. If you have a Patriot subscription, you may want to log in to take advantage of some of the perks, like removing ads, writing articles, and the ability to customize your profile.

  1. Click the blue button in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  2. You will be asked for two pieces of information:
    • The email address you used to register for your Galatea account.
    • Your password. If you can't remember it, learn how to reset your password here.
  3. Once you have finished inputting your email address and password, click the blue button at the bottom of the page.
  4. If your email or password is incorrect, you will receive an error message that says: "ERROR: Invalid email or password." In this case, either try again (double-check to make sure you haven't made any mistakes!) or reset your password.

If you are redirected away from the login page, that means you are successfully logged in!
