Set my status

Users who have an active Patriot Tier subscription can set their status. This can say whatever you like, provided it is 100 characters or less and abides by our Content Policy.

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Click the link in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  3. In the "About You" section, locate the field under "Status". If this is your first time setting your status, you will see a What's up? placeholder in the field. If you have previously set a status, your previous status will be displayed in the field.
  4. If you have previously set a status, you must first delete the old one. You can do this by clicking in the field, pressing the Ctrl + A keys at the same time, then pressing Del.
  5. Type your new status into the box. Your status can be a maximum of 100 characters long.
  6. When you are finished, click the blue button.

If you have successfully set your status, you will see a message that says "Status successfully updated!". You will also see your new status in the "Status" field.
