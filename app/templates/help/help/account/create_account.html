Create an account

You need a user account to do certain things on Galatea, such as commenting on articles, following and messaging users, and receiving email notifications. Luckily, making an account is free and easy!

  1. Click the red button in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  2. You will be asked for five pieces of information:
    • Your email address, so we can contact you if necessary.
    • A password, preferably longer than 8 characters and unique to this website.
    • Your username, which is your identity on this site. (Think of it like a name that preserves your anonymity.) This must be unique.
    • Year of birth, so we know you're old enough to use the site.
    • Gender, so we can show you relevant content we think you'd like.
  3. Once you have finished inputting your information, click the blue button at the bottom of the page.
  4. Congratulations! You now have a Galatea account!
