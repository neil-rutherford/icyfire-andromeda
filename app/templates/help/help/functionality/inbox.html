Check my inbox

You can see messages other users have sent you by visiting your inbox.

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Click the link in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  3. Click on messages in your inbox to open them. Unread messages are shown in white, while read messages are shown in light grey.
