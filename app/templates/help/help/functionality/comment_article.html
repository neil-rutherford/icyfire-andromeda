Comment on an article

All users can comment on articles. This is a great way to share your thoughts and contribute to the discussion. Please be mindful of our Content Policy.

  1. Log in to your account, if you haven't already.
  2. Navigate to the article you would like to comment on.
  3. Scroll to the "Comments" section toward the bottom of the page.
  4. Type your comment into the box that says What do you think?. Click the blue button when you are finished.

If your comment was posted successfully, you will be redirected back to the article. You will see a message that says "Your comment was posted!".
