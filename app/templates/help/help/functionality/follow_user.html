Follow a user

If you think a user is interesting, you can follow them. This means you want to see their content and activity on your home page.

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Go to the user's profile page by clicking on their username or profile picture.
  3. In the left column under the user's profile picture, click the blue button.

If you successfully follow the user, you will see a message that says "You are now following [USERNAME]."
