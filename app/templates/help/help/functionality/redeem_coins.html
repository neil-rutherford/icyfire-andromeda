Redeem coins for Patriot Tier subscription

As the community interacts with one of your articles, you will receive coins. You may redeem 500 coins for 30 days of Patriot Tier subscription.

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Click the link in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  3. Scroll to the "Coins" subsection of the "Manage your account" section. Click the grey button.

If the transaction was successful, you will see a message that says "Transaction successful. You have spent 500 coins, and your subscription has been extended by 30 days."
