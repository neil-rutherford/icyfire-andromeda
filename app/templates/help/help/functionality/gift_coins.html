Send coins to friends

As the community interacts with one of your articles, you will receive coins that can be redeemed for a Patriot Tier subscription. You may gift coins to other users at any time for any reason.

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Click the link in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  3. Scroll to the "Coins" subsection of the "Manage your account" section. Click the grey button.
  4. In the pop-up, fill out the recipient's username and how many coins you would like to give. If you'd like, you may also leave a message.
  5. When you are finished, click the blue button at the bottom of the pop-up.

If the gift was successful, you will see a message that says "You have successfully gifted [AMOUNT] coins to [USERNAME]."
