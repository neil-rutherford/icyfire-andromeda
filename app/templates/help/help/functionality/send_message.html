Send a message

If you would like to speak privately with a user, you may send them a message.

  1. Log in, if you haven't already.
  2. Go to the user's profile page by clicking on their username or profile picture.
  3. In the left column under the user's profile picture, click the blue outlined button.
  4. In the pop-up, fill out the subject and message body fields. Please note that the subject line has a 50 character limit, and the message body has a 300 character limit.
  5. When you are finished, click the blue button.

If your message is successfully sent, you will see a message that says "Message sent!".
