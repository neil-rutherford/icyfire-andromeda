Write an article

Users who have an active Patriot Tier subscription can write articles. This is a great way to share your thoughts and contribute to the discussion. Please be mindful of our Content Policy.

  1. Log in to an acocunt with an active Patriot subscription, if you haven't already.
  2. Click the blue link in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  3. The process is divided into two parts -- deciding how the article will look, and writing the body of the article.
  4. Part 1: Deciding how the article will look
    • As you fill in the fields, a preview will become visible on the top of the page. This is how your article will look on the main page.
    • Adjust the fields until you are satisfied with the preview.
    • When you are finished, click the blue button at the bottom of the page.
  5. Part 2: Writing the article
    • You will see the preview from Part 1 above the article. This is for your reference only.
    • Write your article in the text box with the placeholder What's on your mind?.
    • When you are finished, click the blue button at the bottom of the page.

After your article has successfully been published, you will be redirected to the article. You will see a message that says "Your article is now live!".

As the community interacts with your article, you will earn coins. Coins can be redeemed for Patriot subscription time or gifted to other users.
